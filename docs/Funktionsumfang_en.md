# Scope of Functions: ownERP Server Setup & Maintenance

## Summary

A turnkey, secure-from-day-one server foundation for your ownERP that largely maintains itself in everyday operation. Your data is backed up automatically, your connections stay permanently encrypted and reachable – without you having to worry about the technical details.

## Key Benefits for Users

- **Ready to use immediately** Your server is fully set up and productive from day one – no lengthy preparation, no waiting for manual configuration.
- **Backed up automatically** Your business data is saved regularly and space-efficiently, encrypted on request – older backups are cleaned up on their own, so storage space never becomes a problem.
- **Permanently secure and reachable** Encrypted connections are renewed automatically and protected against attacks, so your application stays trustworthy and fast to reach at all times.
- **Failure-resistant** A single issue at a single address never brings down the entire server – your remaining services keep running without interruption.
- **Low maintenance** Updates and upkeep happen quickly, predictably and gently, so day-to-day operation continues almost unnoticed.

## Functions in Detail

- **Turnkey setup:** The server is fully prepared and handed over in a single pass – ready to use, without you having to lift a finger.
- **Automatic data backup:** Your data is saved regularly, strongly compressed and encrypted on request. Before every backup, the system checks whether enough space is available, so no incomplete backups are created.
- **Self-cleaning storage:** Outdated backups are removed automatically after an adjustable retention period – storage stays permanently tidy.
- **Settings backed up too:** Not only your data, but also the central server settings and security credentials are saved – in an emergency, everything is restored quickly.
- **Predictable updates:** Multiple systems can be updated in one orderly pass, either fully or only in selected parts – gently and traceably.
- **End-to-end encryption:** All connections are encrypted, and the credentials required for this are renewed automatically and in good time – entirely without your involvement.
- **Built-in failure resistance:** If the system detects a problem at a single address, only that one is temporarily taken offline and reported, while everything else keeps running.
- **Optimised reachability:** Behind the scenes, name resolution is tuned for speed and reliability, so your services stay quick and stable to address.
- **Suitable for corporate networks:** Even in secured company networks with special access routes, the server can be operated reliably.
- **Efficient, low-error operation:** Recurring maintenance tasks are prepared so they can be handled quickly and with little risk of operating errors.
- **Self-maintaining foundation:** The setup detects and corrects typical pitfalls on its own and guards against faulty input – noticeably reducing the maintenance effort.

## Use Cases

- **New ownERP server:** You receive a fully prepared, secured environment and can go productive with no lead time.
- **Ongoing operation and maintenance:** Updates and upkeep run in an orderly, gentle way, so your daily business is not interrupted.
- **Emergency and recovery:** Thanks to regular, complete backups, data and settings are restored quickly in an emergency – downtime stays minimal.
- **Operation in a corporate network:** Even behind special network access routes, the server works reliably and stays securely reachable.
- **Multiple systems in parallel:** If you run several applications on one server, they are backed up and maintained together and in an orderly fashion – with consistently high availability.
